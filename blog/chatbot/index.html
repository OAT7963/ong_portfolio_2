Chatbot AI using Neural networks - Education pathway in Malaysia

Chatbot AI using Neural networks - Education pathway in Malaysia

Published on May 12, 2019 by Ong Aun Ter

Introduction and Project Motivation

Majority of the youngsters today (including myself) do not have a clear pathway what they wan to specialize in after their academic career. The fault is in my honest opinion the industry is moving too fast and our country’s education is not keeping up. The similar education system is the ones that is similar to the one in 1950s etc (need more research).

What good is organizing a career fair if the secondary school system does not expose students to real world applications through day to day subjects. Where the schools don’t list down the availability of Universities in Malaysia and the available subjects. For example, how are students exposed to mass communication, environmental science, computer science throughout their time in secondary schools. This will produce many directionless individuals once they’re out of high school.

High school students are expected to head into college 3 - 8 months after they obtained their secondary school certificate. In many cases, passion or interest cannot be built in such short amount of time, it should be nurtured and ultimately developed into something meaningful for the individual.

Progress

My chatbot AI has been built, what’s left is to feed in the data of each and every pathway to their interest in Malaysia. An example of the conversation would be:

Bot: Hi, welcome to (appname)! Tell me what are your interests I will shortlist a few university courses of your choice!

User: My interests are hard science, I think I’d like to major in physics

Bot: Sure, these are the universities that are offering Physics. University of Malaya, TAR UC, UTP

This is just an example, I am in the progress of building the data set

Only the chatbot has been built, it took me about 2 days to build the chatbot using Neural Networks. I suspect the data will take me 2-3 months to complete.
